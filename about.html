About The Bayou

The Bayou Lounge is inspired by the smoky jazz bars and cigar lounges of 1910s New Orleans. We bring together velvet, brass, and blues in perfect harmony. Located in Shrewsbury, we recreate that rich, cozy feel with soft seats, warm lights, and the gentle smell of oak and spice. Enjoy handmade drinks and live jazz music. The Bayou is a place to relax and enjoy something special. Every detail takes you back to a time of rhythm and charm, where each night brings a new story.
