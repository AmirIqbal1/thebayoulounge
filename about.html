About

The Bayou Lounge is like the jazz bars of 1910s New Orleans. We’re in Shrewsbury and have soft seats, golden lights, and the smell of oak and spice. Come for cocktails and live jazz. It’s a place to relax and enjoy the music, where every night is a new story.
